--- title: Filte(red) for (RED) layout: posts categories: projects permalink: /projects/filtered --- At Square's College Code Camp in August 2014, participants were asked to build an app that raised awareness for the charity (RED).

Inspired by the success of that summer's ALS Ice Bucket Challenge on social media, my partner Bethany Davis and I built Filte(red): a tool that overlays (RED)'s iconic red brackets onto a user-uploaded photo, and allows the resulting image to be exported to Twitter.

We ultimately won the hackathon, selected as the best project that represented (RED) and helped it achieve its goals.
